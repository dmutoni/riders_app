# Riders application

Welcome to the Rider's Application, a comprehensive mobile app designed to provide an easy and efficient way for users to book rides, manage their trips, and track their rides in real-time. This README file will guide you through the features, and installation process, and provide a visual overview of the app.


## Feautures 

1. **User Authentication:** Secure login and registration process.
2. **Real-time Ride Tracking:** Track your ride in real-time with live updates.
3. **Multiple Payment Methods:** Choose from a variety of payment options including credit cards, PayPal, and cash.
4. **Ride History:** Access your previous rides and details at any time.
5. **Easy Booking Process:** Seamless booking experience with a few taps.
6. **Interactive Map:** View your current location and destination on the map.
7. **Complain system:** Give us feedback on how you found the vehicle.
